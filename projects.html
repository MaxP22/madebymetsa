Soundcloud

check out my work here

I create music as a way to release my emotions. I find that when I get tired of school and of coding, I fall back on music as a way of still being productive and creative. I use ableton as my software of choice. I self taught my self everything I know, I learned through tutorials and the internet, also by discovering things on my own. Music has been a great way to connect with people as well. I've met friends from all across the globe, and all it takes is a simple message or twitter shout to get someones attention to create a bond. As well as music, I've been able to channel my graphic design into my pages. This is a continuing project that I hope to sustain for many years to come because it brings me such joy.

I do hope you enjoy what you hear.

Graphic Design

check out some of my art

Graphic design has always been a passion of mine. I took two classes in high school that went over basic Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop techniques, and I fell in love. I love to create posters, logos, banners etc etc. I could go on and on. As well as with the classes, I would go in every day at lunch and talk with my professor about techniques to use and how to apply certain things and gain an eye for good design. This really meshes well with the User Experince minor I am pursing, because I can code what I want to see, and that is incredible. I hope you enjoy the art that is uploaded, check back periodically to see if I updated it.
